Gaurav Tiwari – Biography

About Gaurav Tiwari

Indian Paranormal investigator

Gaurav Tiwari was a well-known Indian paranormal investigator and the founder of the Indian Paranormal Society (IPS). He dedicated his life to promoting scientific thinking and rational awareness, especially in areas surrounded by fear, myths, and superstition.

Early Life

Gaurav Tiwari was born on 2 September.From a young age,Gaurav tiwari was trained as a pilot in the united states and successfully completed his aviation training. During this period, he experienced unexplained incidents that sparked his interest in the paranormal, eventually leading him to become india's most well-known paranormal investigator. he showed deep interest in science, psychology, and unexplained phenomena. Instead of blind belief, he encouraged questioning and logical thinking.He is not a paranormal investigator,but also a strong advocate of scientific skepticism.

Work & Research

He founded the Indian Paranormal Society (IPS) to investigate paranormal claims using scientific methods. His work focused on educating people, exposing fake claims, and reducing fear through knowledge.His main work was to investigate places that people believed were haunted and to study unusual incidents related to paranormal claims. He and his team visited houses, offices, hospitals, and historical locations to examine these reports. He also helped people who were afraid of supernatural activities by explaining the possible reasons behind such experiences. In addition, he trained students interested in paranormal investigation and worked through television programs and public events to spread awareness and educate people about this field.

RESEARCH

Haunted places research – He investigated houses, buildings, hospitals, and historical sites where people reported paranormal activities. Environmental research – He studied factors like electromagnetic fields (EMF), temperature changes, and sound disturbances to find scientific explanations. Psychological research – He examined how fear, stress, imagination, or hallucinations can affect people and make them believe in supernatural events. Case investigations – He researched individual cases where families or individuals claimed unusual or unexplained experiences. Public awareness research – He worked to understand common myths and beliefs about ghosts and tried to explain them through logic and science. CASES: Gaurav Tiwari investigated thousands of paranormal cases across India, many of which were explained through scientific and logical research rather than supernatural cause

IPS tean

The Indian Paranormal Society (IPS), founded by Gaurav Tiwari, was supported by a dedicated team of trained investigators and researchers. Key members included Gaurav Tiwari as the founder and lead investigator, along with Siddharth Bantval, Rith Deb, Waqar Raj, Meghna Porwal, and Mohan Kattimani. The team worked together to investigate paranormal cases across India, using scientific methods and research to find logical explanations for unusual experiences, educate people, and reduce fear of the supernatural.

Core Belief

One of his most well-known thoughts was:

“Knowledge cancels fear.”

Legacy

Gaurav Tiwari is remembered as a rational thinker who inspired many young minds to question superstition and rely on science and reason. His work continues to influence people interested in logical inquiry and awareness.
